Planned vs Reactive vs Blocked · Reactive work is real work — tracked for visibility, never penalised against vision targets
How to read this

Planned — Work that was anticipated in the sprint or cycle plan.

Reactive / Unplanned — Production fires, urgent client requests, last-minute asks. Real work. High reactive % signals a planning gap, not an effort gap.

Blocked / Waiting — Time spent waiting on a dependency, approval, or external input. Flags process bottlenecks.

Vision alignment is calculated on planned hours only. Reactive and blocked hours are excluded from the denominator.
